  1. $\underset{x\rightarrow3}{lim}\,\ \frac{{{x}^{2}}+x-12}{x-3}\ = $

    $\frac{{{3}^{2}}+3-12}{3-3}=\frac{0}{0}$

    Indeterminate form: Factor, reduce, take the limit again.

    $\underset{x\rightarrow3}{lim}\,\ \frac{(x+4)(x-3)}{x-3}$

    $=\underset{x\rightarrow3}{lim}\,\ \left( x+4 \right)$

    $=3+4=$

    $7$

  2. $\underset{x\rightarrow1}{lim}\,\ \frac{{{x}^{2}}+x-12}{x-3}\ = $

    $\frac{{{1}^{2}}+1-12}{1-3}=\frac{-10}{-2}=$

    $5$

  3. $\underset{x\rightarrow10}{lim}\,\ 10 =$

    $10$

  4. $\underset{x\rightarrow4}{lim}\,\ 3{{x}^{2}}-5x = $

    $3{{(4)}^{2}}-5(4)$

    $=3(16)-20$

    $=48-20=$

    $28$

  5. $\underset{x\rightarrow0}{lim}\,\ \frac{4x-5{{x}^{2}}}{x-1} = $

    $\frac{4(0)-5{{(0)}^{2}}}{0-1}=\frac{0}{-1}=$

    $0$

    $\frac{{{1}^{2}}-1}{1-1}=\frac{0}{0}$
